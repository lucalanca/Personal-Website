    • 2013 – 2014

      Serei As Chaves

      This was my first (and only, for now) startup. I co-founded it while I was still in college. It is an online platform to bet in euromillions in syndicates. Syndicates can be public or private and we take care of the bets submission, and prize distribution. Users are able to participate in large syndicates multiplying their chances of winning or invite their friends into a private syndicate and automate the whole process of managing a syndicate. I created all the front end and helped in some back end related tasks. I was also involved in many business decisions and work streams.

      Tags: Ruby on Rails, HTML, SASS, Javascript, jQuery.
